# Prompt: Caption Generator (Feed / Story)

Generate an Instagram caption for the following post.

## Brand Profile
{brand_profile}

## Post Details
- Format: {format}
- Theme: {theme}
- Hashtags to append: {hashtags}

## Instructions

Write a compelling caption that:
1. Matches the brand tone of voice exactly
2. Starts with a hook (question, bold statement, or relatable opener)
3. Has 2–4 short paragraphs or a list
4. Ends with a clear call to action (e.g., "Save this for later", "Tag a friend", "What do you think? Comment below")
5. Uses emojis naturally (not excessively — 2–4 per paragraph max)
6. The hashtags are already included — append them at the very end after two line breaks

## Output format

Output ONLY the caption text followed by two newlines and the hashtag string.
Do not include any explanation or markdown.

Caption for:
Theme: {theme}
